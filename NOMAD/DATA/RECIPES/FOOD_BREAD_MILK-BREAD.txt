#SECTION:INGREDIENTS
    Tangzhong:
        -2x tbsp Flour, 20g
        -2x tbsp Water, 27g
        -4½x tbsp Milk, 60g

    Yeast Mixture:
        -1x tbsp active Instant Yeast, 10g
        -2x tbsp Lukewarm Water, 20g
        -10g Sugar

    Dough:
        -Tangzhong
        -Yeast Mixture
        -AP Flour, 380g
        -Sugar, 50g
        -Salt, 3g
        -Milk, 130g
        -1 Large Egg
        -3 tbsp Butter
#ENDSECTION

#SECTION:INSTRUCTIONS

1.Prepping Tangzhong
In a saucepan, add the tangzhong ingredients and cook, continuing to stir over low heat until a paste forms. Should be thick and you will be able to pick all of it up with a spoon.

2.Prepping Yeast Mixture
Add the active dry yeast into the lukewarm water, mix and allow to sit for 10 minutes.

3.Forming Dough
Combine your yeast mixture and tangzhong along with the remaining dough ingredients in a large bowl. Bring together and form dough before kneeding for 10-15 minutes.

4.First Proof
Cover dough and allow to proof for 2hrs.

5.Forming Loaf
Divide the dough into three equal pieces. Roll out each before folding and place in a loaf tin lined with baking parchment.

6.Second Proof
Cover and allow to sit in the loaf for 45 minutes.

7.Bake
Bake uncovered in a oven preheated to 165°C for 30-33 minutes.

#ENDSECTION
