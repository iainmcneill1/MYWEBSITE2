#SECTION:INGREDIENTS
    Protein:
        - 1x Chicken Breast or 3x Chicken Thighs

    Fats/Oils:
        - 1.5x tbsp Olive Oil

    Spice Mix:
        - 1/4x tsp Cumin
        - 1/4x tsp Ground Coriander
        - 1x tsp Ground Ginger
        - 1/2x tsp Ground Cinnamon
        - 1x tsp Paprika
        - 1/2x tsp Salt
        - 2-3x ground Cardamom Pods

    Misc: 
        - Pinch of Sugar
        - Juice of half a Lemon
        - 1x tsp Greek Yogurt
        - 1x clove of Garlic

#ENDSECTION

#SECTION:INSTRUCTIONS

1. Prep the chicken and spices
Cut the chicken into small pieces and mix together with together all Spices and Misc in a bowl.

2. Marinade
Allow to marinade minimum 2 hours (Recommended 24hrs).

3. Cooking
Either fry in a pan with a little olive oil, or grill in the oven at 180 for 23 mins.

#ENDSECTION
