#SECTION:INGREDIENTS
    Protein:
        - 2x Chicken Breast

    Fruits/Vegetables: 
        - 1x White Onion 
        - 3x Garlic Cloves
        - 2-3x Small Tomatoes
        - 1x Juice of Lemon
        - 2-3 Dates (Halved)


    Fats/Oils:
        - 2x tbsp Olive Oil

    Stock:
        - 240ml Chicken Stock

    Spice Mix:
        - 1x tsp Cumin
        - 1x tsp Ground Coriander
        - 1x tsp Ground Ginger
        - 1x tsp Ground Cinnamon
        - 1/2x tsp Turmeric 
        - 1x tsp Paprika

    Misc: 
        - 1x tbsp Honey
        - 1x tbsp Tomato Paste
#ENDSECTION

#SECTION:INSTRUCTIONS

1. Prep the chicken and spices
Pat the chicken dry, season lightly with salt and pepper.
In a tagine, Dutch oven, or slow cooker insert, heat olive oil over medium heat.
Brown chicken on both sides until lightly golden (about 3–4 minutes per side). Remove and set aside.

2. Build the flavor base
In the same pot, add onions and cook until softened (5–7 minutes).
Stir in garlic and all spices (cumin, coriander, ginger, cinnamon, turmeric, paprika). Cook 1 minute to bloom the spices.

3. Deglaze and mix liquids
Add tomato paste, honey, lemon juice, and chicken stock. Stir well, scraping any browned bits off the bottom.

4. Combine and cook low and slow
Combine all components in large dutch oven or tagine, cook low and slow. Recommended at oven 90°C for 4 hours, before increasing to 140°C for 2 hours.
#ENDSECTION
